    About Crypto Brick Breaker

    Welcome to Crypto Brick Breaker: Blockchain Battle! This is a classic arcade game reimagined with a vibrant cryptocurrency theme. Your mission is to break through layers of crypto blocks, each representing a different digital asset, and build your portfolio value.

    Game Elements:

    • Crypto Blocks: Each block you destroy is a cryptocurrency. Break them to earn "Portfolio Value". Watch out for different types, some are tougher than others!
    • Mining Rig (Paddle): Control your advanced mining rig to bounce the "Transaction Ball" and break crypto blocks.
    • Transaction Ball: Your primary tool for interacting with the blockchain. Keep it in play!
    • Wallet Balance (Lives): Represents your available funds or attempts. Don't let the Transaction Ball fall out of the network!
    • Blockchain Depth (Level): As you clear all crypto blocks, you advance to the next "Blockchain Depth", facing new challenges and more valuable assets.
